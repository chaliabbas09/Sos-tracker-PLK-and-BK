Tim Hortons - double espresso extraction times

The time starts when you hear the second click in the espresso machine, then stop when you hear the click at the end of the extraction process.
Target extraction time is 26 seconds (+/- 3 seconds). Please check the 3 espresso and if out the allowed time range, measure the opportunity.

Some restaurant participate in the trail where espresso extraction times might be different. Please check the exception tracer before measuring this opportunity.

How to measure Popeyes dine-in speed of service
  1. Click New Guest button when customer paid for their order (receipt is printed). It could be either kiosk or front counter POS.
  2. Then click on sandwich icon when customer receives their order.
  3. Once you click on the meal icon, app will calculate service time for that transaction and update the average speed of service time based on number measured transactions. Results will be displayed in fields on the top of the form.
  4. Measured average service times are color coded:
    Green = ON TARGET (below 2:15), ORANGE = PARTIAL POINTS AWARDED (between 2:16 and 3:15), RED = ZERO POINTS AWARDED (above 3:15)
  5. Click on trash icon if you want to delete an of the customer (i.e. when you lost track of that customer)
  6. If icon is active, you can overwrite payment of food received time.
  7. Click on reload page button if you want to reload the page (clear all the data on the page). Warning - all the measured times will be lost!!!
How to measure TH Dine-in Speed of Service time
  1. Click New Guest button when customer paid for their order (when received the receipt)
  2. Click on the donut icon when guest received the order.
  3. Once you click on the donut icon, it will calculate transaction service time. Average service time will be updated based on the number of measured transactions. Results will be displayed in fields on the top of the form.
  4. Measured average service times are color coded:
    Green = ON TARGET (below 2:30), ORANGE = PARTIAL POINTS AWARDED (between 2:31 and 4:00) , RED = ZERO POINTS AWARDED (above 4:00)
  5. Click on trash icon if you want to delete that guest/transaction.
  6. If icon is active, you can overwrite corresponding time.
  7. Click on reload page button if you want to reload the page (clear all the data on the page). Warning - all the measured times will be lost!!!
How to measure double espresso extraction times
  1. Click New coffee button when you hear second click on the espresso machine
  2. Click on coffee cup once extraction finishes (you should hear another click).
  3. Once you click on coffee cup icon, app will calculate espresso extraction time for that espresso and then recalculate the average extraction time based on number measured shots. Results will be displayed in the field on the top of the form.
  4. Total extraction times and avg extraction are color coded:
    Green = ON TARGET (between 23 and 29 seconds), RED = MORE THAN 3 seconds ABOVE or BELOW TARGET (TARGET = 26 seconds).
  5. Click on trash icon if you want to delete that extraction.
  6. If icon is active, you can overwrite corresponding time.
  7. Click on reload page button if you want to reload the page (clear all the data on the page). Warning - all the measured times will be lost!!!
How to measure Burger King front counter service times
  1. Click New Guest button when Guest receives receipt or cash/card back.
  2. Click on hamburger meal icon when customer receives his/her order.
  3. Once you click hamburger icon, it will calculate front counter service time and it will display it in the field. Average service time will be calculated base on completed transactions. Results will be displayed in the field on the top of the form.
  4. Total service time and avg service time are color coded:
    Green = ON TARGET (below 2:00), ORANGE = PART POINTS AWARDED (between 2:01 and 3:00), RED = ABOVE TARGET (above 3:00)
  5. Click on trash icon if you want to delete that customer/transaction.
  6. If icon is active, you can overwrite corresponding time.
  7. Click on reload page button if you want to reload the page (clear all the data on the page).
